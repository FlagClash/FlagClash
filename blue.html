Blue

Blue is an alternative mode of playing FlagClash. You have a limited number of lives per stage and you have the option of 3 of the Nodes to upgrade to, each has their own Task that needs to be completed to get it. After every 10 stages the challenge would increase since the value of tasks goes from 1 to 2 to 3. Blue is completed once reaching stage 30
